### 1) Game Name:
**Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes - Simon Says Module**

### 2) Game: Description
This game is similar to the classic "Simon Says" toy game, where players must match the sequence of colors that flash. In this module, you need to press the button corresponding to the flashing color using a provided table to determine the correct color to press based on the serial number and the number of strikes.

### 3) How to Play
#### Case 1: If the Serial Number Contains a Vowel

- **IF there are No Strikes:**
  - Red Flash: Press **Blue**
  - Blue Flash: Press **Red**
  - Green Flash: Press **Yellow**
  - Yellow Flash: Press **Green**

- **IF there is 1 Strike:**
  - Red Flash: Press **Yellow**
  - Blue Flash: Press **Green**
  - Green Flash: Press **Blue**
  - Yellow Flash: Press **Red**

- **IF there are 2 Strikes:**
  - Red Flash: Press **Green**
  - Blue Flash: Press **Red**
  - Green Flash: Press **Yellow**
  - Yellow Flash: Press **Blue**

#### Case 2: If the Serial Number Does Not Contain a Vowel

- **IF there are No Strikes:**
  - Red Flash: Press **Blue**
  - Blue Flash: Press **Red**
  - Green Flash: Press **Green**
  - Yellow Flash: Press **Yellow**

- **IF there is 1 Strike:**
  - Red Flash: Press **Red**
  - Blue Flash: Press **Blue**
  - Green Flash: Press **Yellow**
  - Yellow Flash: Press **Green**

- **IF there are 2 Strikes:**
  - Red Flash: Press **Yellow**
  - Blue Flash: Press **Green**
  - Green Flash: Press **Blue**
  - Yellow Flash: Press **Red**
